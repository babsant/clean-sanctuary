Privacy Policy

Clean Sanctuary — Last updated: February 2, 2025

Overview

Clean Sanctuary is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe your personal data should stay personal. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data Stored on Your Device

All of your personal data is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to our servers. This includes:

  • Your profile and preferences (energy level, app tone, home configuration)
  • Cleaning history and completed quests
  • Points, streaks, and progress statistics
  • Room names and cleaning schedules

This data never leaves your device unless you choose to delete the app, at which point it is permanently removed.

Anonymous Community Data

If you unlock the Community feature, we share a small amount of anonymous data to power the community bonfire:

  • An anonymous, randomly generated identifier (not linked to your identity)
  • Points contributed to the community total

This data cannot be used to identify you and is not linked to any personal information.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Your name, email, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Any data from other apps on your device

Third-Party Services

Clean Sanctuary does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by using the "Reset All Data" option in the app's Profile screen. Uninstalling the app will also remove all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

Clean Sanctuary does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at cleansanctuary.app@gmail.com.

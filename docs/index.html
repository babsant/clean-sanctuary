About

Clean Sanctuary helps people with ADHD maintain their living spaces through gentle, step-by-step cleaning quests. No judgment, no overwhelm—just simple guidance when you need it.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's how Clean Sanctuary handles your data:

  • Local storage only: All your personal data (preferences, cleaning history, stats) is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.
  • Anonymous community data: If you participate in the community feature, only anonymous contribution points are shared—no personal information.
  • No tracking: We do not use analytics or tracking services.
  • No ads: Clean Sanctuary does not display advertisements.
